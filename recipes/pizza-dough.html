Pizza Dough

Amazing dough made with a poolish starter. Only dough needed for a Neapolitan Style Pizza!

Poolish Recipe

  • 300gr lukewarm water
  • 5g Dry Yeast
  • 5g Honey
  • 300gr 00 Pizza Flour

Poolish Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients together
  2. Cover and let rest on counter for 1 hour
  3. After 1 hour place into the fridge overnight

Dough Recipe

  • 700gr lukewarm water
  • 40gr Salt
  • 1250gr 00 Pizza Flour
